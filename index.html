About Bons

Water the way it was meant to taste.

Bons started with a simple frustration — water that tasted of plastic, chlorine, or nothing at all. We set out to bottle the opposite: a water so quiet and clean it reminds you why you were thirsty in the first place.

From our highland source to the sealed cap, every drop passes through seven quality checks. It's the kind of obsessive care you'd hope for — and rarely find.

  • BPA-free food-grade PET bottles, fully recyclable.
  • Solar-powered bottling plant — lower footprint per bottle.
  • Same-day bulk delivery across the city, seven days a week.
